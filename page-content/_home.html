Dave Hayes

Computer Science Educator and Advocate

Following a career in the tech sector, I became an educator and have had the privilege of helping others learn in a variety of contexts: university courses, high school courses, summer camps for kids, training for high school teachers, and more.

Now, my primary focus is teaching at the University of Illinois - Chicago, but I also continue to do some work with Chicago Public Schools, with Code.org and with non-school programs for young people in Chicago.
